std::optional< VerifiedToken > axiam::JwksVerifier::verify ( const std::string &  jwt)

Verify a compact JWS.

Ed25519/EdDSA only. On success returns the payload; returns nullopt if the alg is not EdDSA, the kid is unknown, the token is malformed, or the signature does not verify. Does not check exp.
